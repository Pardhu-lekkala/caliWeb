Nurturing Nature

Cali Mykonos is a self-sustaining ecosystem,
a symbiotic relationship between nature and nurture, the constructed and emergent. To preserve the health of our environment, everything we do is designed to drastically reduce our ecological footprint and create lifetimes of exceptional experiences.

Nurturing Nature

Cali Mykonos is a self-sustaining ecosystem, a symbiotic relationship between nature and nurture, the constructed and emergent. To preserve the health of our environment, everything we do is designed to drastically reduce our ecological footprint and create lifetimes of exceptional experiences.

Our Commitment

Seawater Pools

Avoid straining the island's water supply.

Sustainable Materials

Reduce waste and energy consumption.

Planted Roof System

Provide insulation and improve air quality.

Fresh Water Sources

Collect water naturally and minimize our use of the island's water supply.

Wastewater Treatment Systems

Remove impurities and irrigate the surrounding landscape.

Locally Grown & Sourced Ingredients

Boost biodiversity while minimizing waste and pollution.

Our Goal

At Cali, we strive to protect, preserve, and celebrate the connection between people and their environment. To create beautiful moments rooted in our collective past, present, and future.

Our Eletric Fleet

Calming interiors with breathtaking exteriors open up to spacious and completely secluded marble terraces with seawater pools.

What We've Accomplished

A self-sustaining ecosystem that nourishes through nature. Soaring above our private beach, the suites and villas of Cali Mykonos are uniquely situated to enhance the natural surroundings.
