Our strength comes from the enrichment of our community. Through employment opportunities and partnerships—like collaborations with local fashion designers and artists, or sourcing food from local fisherman—we're building a network rooted in the prosperity and continuous growth of the people of Greece.

Whether you work with us or stay with us, everyone here is part of the Cali family. From our indispensable staff, to guests familiar and new, to the locals helping us create lifelong memories, our people are the key to our legacy and impact.

Cali Mykonos is a self-sustaining ecosystem, a symbiotic relationship between nature and nurture, the constructed and emergent. To preserve the health of our environment, everything we do is designed to drastically reduce our ecological footprint and create lifetimes of exceptional experiences.

About Cali

Soaring above our private beach, the suites and villas of Cali Mykonos are uniquely situated to enhance the natural surroundings. Each offers a seawater pool and spacious terrace, creating an effortless flow between indoor and outdoor spaces that invites in the endless Aegean throughout.

Guests are welcome to swim in our infinity pool, eat locally sourced foods at our restaurant, and rebalance at our world-class spa.
