Helmed by Greece’s Michelin-starred chef, Lefteris Lazarou, culinary offerings will be exceptional, with locally-sourced ingredients and fresh seasonal produce picked daily from our garden.

Lazarou's culinary expedition began as a child, accompanying his father in ship kitchens, where he learned to utilize the ingredients of wherever the ship docked. These experiences culminated in a cuisine that’s ever-evolving and varying daily.
