The beauty of nature in balance.

Cali is not imposed on the landscape, but is driven by the natural flow of the terrain. Traditional white structures emerge from rocks and gracefully cascade along the coast, blurring the line between natural and manmade. The architecture listens to its surroundings, adapting to its needs and celebrating its bounty—drawing upon the sea to fill its pools, the surrounding mountains for drinking water, local stone and marble for pathways and structures, the gardens for ingredients, and planted roof systems for insulation and purification.
