Cali Collection
Villa Calliope

  • Indoor Space:  
  • 335 square meters
  • Private Terrace:  
  • 670 square meter

With over 1000 m2 of indoor and outdoor space, the four-bedroom Villa Calliope is unparalleled. The primary suite is set above the rest of the residence, with access to its own expansive terrace, marble-clad en suite, and large seawater infinity pool which flows into a second pool below. Each of the spacious bedrooms feature a COCO-MAT sleep system, custom marble detailing, and breathtaking views. A sheltered lawn with unobstructed sea vistas and private driveway ensure you will want for nothing in this opulent villa, but its the sight of the horizon extending along the pristine Aegean Sea that you will remember, always.
