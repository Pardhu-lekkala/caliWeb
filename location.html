A luxurious hideaway in Mykonos, Greece.

Cali Mykonos sits on the southeastern edge of the island in the discrete coastal village of Kalafati. Nestled between mountains and protected from the seasonal Mykonian winds, the resort is perched above our private beach—a rarity in Mykonos—with unobstructed views of the endless Aegean Sea.

A luxury retreat for those seeking the best of both worlds, Cali Mykonos is a self-sustaining haven of tranquility with the vibrant town of Chora only 12km away—not too close, but not too far, either.

Plane

The resort is a 15-minute (10km) drive from Mykonos Airport, which offers daily flights to several domestic Greece and European destinations including London, Athens, Paris, Rome, and more. From there, guests can take connecting flights to the U.S. and beyond.

Car

We provide private transportation upon request and complimentary shuttle service to and from Mykonos Airport and Chora (the island’s main town).

Ferry

The old and new ferry ports of Mykonos are a 30-minute drive (13km) from the resort, providing travel to remote Greek islands like Tinos, Milos, and Naxos.

Boat

Our fleet of yachts and luxury boats are available for private rides and tours to neighboring beaches, towns, and islands.

Helicopter

Our private helipad on the property offers transfers or journeys upon request.
